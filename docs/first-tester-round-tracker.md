## Round Success Criteria

- at least 3 testers complete yogurt flow
- at least 2 testers complete duplicate-line flow
- at least 2 feedback reports include requestId or errorId
- at least 1 confusing UX issue is identified
- no P0 demo blocker remains open

## Decision After Round

- continue frontend polish
- improve parser or catalog behavior
- prepare real-data plan
- improve deployment or observability
- pause and fix demo blockers
